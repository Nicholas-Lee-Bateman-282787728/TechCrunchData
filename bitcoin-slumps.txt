2015 has not been kind to bitcoin thus far. The cryptocurrency, which has endured a stiff price decline since its late-2013 heights, isback below the $300 mark. Bitcoins total transaction volume, as measured by Blockchain.info, has also slippedin recent days.Theoriesaboundabout why its price has slumped, but there seems to be a lack of consensus in bitcoins enthusiast community as to what is going on.

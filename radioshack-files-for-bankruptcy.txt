While theyve spent the last few years trying to find their roots with things like a dedicated section for Arduino gear*, theyve mostly failed to get bodies back in the store.(* A section which employees never seemed to understand. I bought two Arduinos there over the period of about six months, as I was working on projects and didnt feel like waiting for delivery. Both times, the employee behind the register asked something along the lines of So what is this thing?)This news comes as rumors swirl that RadioShack intends to sell off half of its stores and close the rest.RadioShacks Chapter 11 filing is below:RadioShack Chapter 11 Petition

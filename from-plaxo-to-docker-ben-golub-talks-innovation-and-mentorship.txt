Ben Golub, CEO of Docker, has run companies before including Gluster and Plaxo and held leadership positions at others such as Verisign.In this podcast, Golub talks about picking apricots in Silicon Valley, learnings from the challenging Plaxo journey, his excitement around leading Docker, the current climate for entrepreneurs, and the value of mentorship.From working closely with founder Solomon Hykes to leverage the Docker movement, building a culture that scales, and executing against the company mission to build mass tools of mass innovation, Golub walks listeners through his entrepreneurial journey.Subscribe to this podcast  via iTunes, Stitcher, RSS.
